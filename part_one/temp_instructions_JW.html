You get an initial endowment of {{ C.ENDOWMENT }}. Four different scenarios will be presented. You will be asked to choose between a risky alternative and a safe alternative for each of the four scenarios. Your answers are independent of each other. Remember, there are no right or wrong answers!

After all participants submit their answers, the computer selects one of the scenarios for your final payoff. You will learn the randomly selected scenario and its outcome at the end of the experiment. Before starting the experiment, you will have to correctly answer two control questions. They ensure that you understood the experiment's rules.

You should always decide as if your choice will be implemented!
